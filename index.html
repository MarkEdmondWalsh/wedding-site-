Our Story

We met on a rainy afternoon in October 2019 at a small coffee shop downtown. What started as a chance encounter over a shared table quickly turned into hours of conversation, laughter, and the unmistakable feeling that something special had begun.

Three years, countless adventures, and one very nervous proposal later, we're thrilled to invite you to celebrate the next chapter of our journey together. We can't wait to share this day with the people who mean the most to us.

Travel & Accommodation

Accommodation

Adare has a range of accommodation options. We recommend booking early as the village is popular in summer.

The Dunraven Arms (reception venue) and nearby B&Bs are great options.

Getting to Adare

Adare is located on the N21, approximately 16km from Limerick City.

Shannon Airport is the nearest airport, about 40 minutes away.

On the Day

The church and reception venue are both in Adare village, a short walk apart.

Taxis can be arranged for the evening.
